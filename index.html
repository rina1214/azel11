A Place to Feel at Home

Koozi Place Milk Tea is a cozy and relaxing hangout spot where friends, families, and milk tea lovers come together to enjoy refreshing drinks and good vibes.

Our goal is to create a comfortable place where every sip brings happiness and every visit feels like home. Located in a peaceful area, Koozi Place is perfect for bonding moments, study sessions, or simply unwinding after a long day.

With its chill ambiance and friendly service, we aim to give our customers not just drinks but a memorable experience.
